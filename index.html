K-12 Pathways: every row is one complete journey a student takes through the district: elementary → middle → high school, based on the district's official feeder patterns. Scores read Elem → MS → HS so you can see the whole trajectory at a glance. The ↗ / ↘ arrow compares the high school value with the elementary value. Feeder patterns are the typical path; boundary changes, choice programs, and waivers mean individual journeys can differ.

WASchoolLens is an independent dashboard created by a parent to help families, teachers, and administrators compare schools more clearly. Not affiliated with OSPI or any school district.
Data: OSPI Washington State Report Card (data.wa.gov), school year. Some values are suppressed by OSPI to protect student privacy (N<10 / DAT rules).
Feeder patterns reflect district-published typical pathways and may change with boundary adjustments.
